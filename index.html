the manifesto

The desk was
never the point.

Coding agents were born tethered — to a desk, a screen, a chair you have to stay in. flaude cuts the cord. Your agent now lives on the one device that's always with you: start runs, watch live previews, drop a photo into context, and steer Claude Code straight from your phone.
